About BrainFlex

In the United States, more than five million Americans suffers from Alzheimer's or another form of dementia. However, scientists have found that daily mental training can help slow the effects of dementia by up to ten years. BrainFlex was created by high school seniors Sarah Gutierrez and Madison Tully for their final project for the 2016 Girls Who Code Summer Immersion Program.
